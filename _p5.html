Challenge 3 — Manual coordination around suppliers, PO rescheduling, and inventory
The Osborn team is spending significant time each day on manual coordination: chasing suppliers for delivery updates via email, rescheduling POs in SAP when dates slip, and reconciling inventory positions in spreadsheets. This is consuming capacity that should be focused on exceptions and decisions — not data movement. Agentforce Operations automates these process loops end-to-end, from trigger to resolution, with a full audit trail and humans only where judgment is actually required.
